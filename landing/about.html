About MyWaterSessions

MyWaterSessions is the free community speed ranking and spot guide for windsurfing, kitesurfing, wing foiling, SUP and wakeboarding.

🌊 Launch the app

What is MyWaterSessions?

MyWaterSessions is a free community platform where windsurfers, kitesurfers, wingfoilers, SUP paddlers and wakeboarders record their GPS sessions and compare their speed. You record a session on a Garmin watch (or upload a FIT file from any watch), and your best 2-second and 30-second speeds, longest run and distance land in a global, per-spot ranking automatically.

How does it work?

  • Record – use the free WaterSession Garmin Connect IQ app on your watch, or upload a FIT file (e.g. from an Apple Watch).
  • Compare – your session uploads to your account and enters the community ranking, overall or per spot, with a fair GPS trust score.
  • Explore – check the spot guide with photos, a 3-day wind forecast and your own personal records and Performance Index.

Which sports does it support?

Five water sports: windsurfing, kitesurfing, wing foiling, SUP and wakeboarding. Each has its own rankings and records.

What makes it different?

  • 100% free – no premium, no paywall. A genuine free alternative to paid GPS speed-surfing sites.
  • More filters – rank by spot, wind strength, board/gear, rider weight and foil size, so you compare with truly similar riders.
  • Not just for pros – a fair Performance Index (fast with little wind and a small sail) and a GPS trust score give beginners something to chase too.
  • Groups, Spot-TV and a spot guide with live wind forecast.

Who is it for?

Anyone on the water who wants to track speed and find conditions – from first-timers to record chasers, across windsurf, kite, wing, SUP and wakeboard.
