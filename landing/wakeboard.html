How it works — with any watch

Record on a Garmin with our free Connect IQ app and the session uploads automatically. No Garmin? Just upload a FIT / GPX / TCX file from any other watch or phone app — Apple Watch, Suunto, COROS, or a Garmin Connect / Strava export. Speed, GPS track and every metric above are computed for you.
