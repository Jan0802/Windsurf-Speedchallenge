🌊 Open the windsurf ranking

MyWaterSessions is a free community speed ranking & spot guide for windsurfing — part of a platform that also covers kitesurfing, wing foiling, SUP and wakeboarding. Record a session, upload it, and your results appear automatically in the ranking, per spot, with a fair trust score.

What gets tracked

⚡ Speed

Your best 2 s and 30 s speeds — the classic speedsurfing benchmarks.

📏 Distance disciplines

Fastest 500 m and nautical mile, like the established speed-surf categories.

🚩 Runs & distance

Longest run and total distance per session.

🛡️ Trust score

A fair plausibility check on every GPS session, so the ranking stays honest.

How it works — with any watch

Record on a Garmin with our free Connect IQ app and the session uploads to your account automatically. No Garmin? Just upload a FIT / GPX / TCX file from any other watch or phone app — Apple Watch, Suunto, COROS, or a Garmin Connect / Strava export. Speed, GPS track and all the metrics above are computed for you.

Why windsurfers use it

  • 100% free — no premium, no paywall on the ranking.
  • Per-spot rankings and a personal performance index (fast with little wind & a small sail).
  • Spot guide + 3-day wind forecast with a “worth it” rating — browse spots.
  • Groups & community to compare within your crew, plus a Spot-TV live screen for your club or café.
