Track your speed, climb the ranking

Works with any GPS watch: record on a Garmin and it uploads automatically through our app — or upload a FIT / GPX / TCX file from any other watch or phone (Apple Watch, Suunto, COROS, Strava export…). Your top 2 s, 30 s, 500 m and nautical-mile speeds, plus longest run and distance, land in the community ranking automatically.

🏆

Speed rankings

Top 2 s, 30 s, 500 m and nautical-mile speeds, longest run and total distance, plus highest jump & airtime (wind sports & wakeboard) – overall or per spot, with a fair trust score.

📈

Your results

Personal records, a performance index (fast with little wind & a small sail) and your exact rank per spot.

🗺️

Spot guide & forecast

Spot descriptions, photos and a 3-day wind forecast with a “worth it” rating and thermal/sea-breeze potential. Browse all spots →

⌚

Any watch · Garmin auto-upload

Record on a Garmin with live speed screens – it uploads to your account automatically. No Garmin? Upload a FIT / GPX / TCX file from any watch or phone.

👥

Groups & community

Create or join groups, compare within your crew and see new records in the news feed.

📺

Spot-TV

A full-screen live leaderboard for your café, shop or club – with a QR code to join today’s ranking.

📊

Session analysis

A speed-over-time curve for every session, your longest planing run, and a gybe & tack analysis that shows how much speed you carry through each turn – see exactly where a manoeuvre drops off the plane.

🎽

Sail & fin advisor

Per-hour sail size from your own quiver (your weight, the wind & your board) plus a fin-size recommendation for your setup – guidance Windy can’t give, because it doesn’t know your gear.

Who is it for?

Not just for the top pro chasing world records. MyWaterSessions is for the ambitious hobby rider who loves their sport, wants to watch themselves get better, and enjoys sharing sessions with friends. Beat your own personal best, see where you stand at your home spot, and celebrate a good day on the water together – whatever your level.
