📖 How it works — read the full guide

Track your speed, climb the ranking

Record a session on your Garmin watch (or upload a FIT file from any watch, e.g. Apple Watch). Your best 2-second and 30-second speeds, longest run and distance land in the community ranking automatically.

🏆

Speed rankings

Top 2 s and 30 s speeds, longest run and total distance – overall or per spot, with a fair trust score.

📈

Your results

Personal records, a performance index (fast with little wind & a small sail) and your exact rank per spot.

🗺️

Spot guide & forecast

Spot descriptions, photos and a 3-day wind forecast with a “worth it” rating and thermal/sea-breeze potential.

⌚

Garmin watch app

Record on your wrist with live speed screens; the session uploads to your account automatically.

👥

Groups & community

Create or join groups, compare within your crew and see new records in the news feed.

📺

Spot-TV

A full-screen live leaderboard for your café, shop or club – with a QR code to join today’s ranking.
