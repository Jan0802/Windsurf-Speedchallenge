Beta 0.9

July 2026

📈 Session analysis (new)
  • Speed over time – every session now has a speed curve reconstructed from your GPS track, with the planing threshold, your runs shaded (longest highlighted) and your real top speed (GPS spikes filtered out).
  • Longest run – your longest continuous planing run is now recorded for watch sessions too (and we back-filled it for existing ones), so it counts in the rankings.
🔄 Gybe analysis (new)
  • Your manoeuvres, analysed. We detect your gybes & tacks and mark them on the speed curve with a traffic-light for how much speed you kept.
  • Pick a single gybe to break it into four phases – approach (−50 m), apex, rig-flip (+10 m) and exit (+50 m) – so you see exactly where a gybe drops off the plane.
  • Something no big tracker shows: not just that you rode, but how well your manoeuvres run. With the watch app update the phases are computed on the watch second-by-second for exact values.

Beta 0.8.6

July 2026

🗺️ Fix a spot's suitability yourself
  • Correct the “Who is this spot for?” rating – set a spot's level, good-to-learn, water type, wind, hazards and per-sport suitability directly in the backoffice, overriding the automatic AI rating. Mark a calm home lake as beginner-friendly / flat-water and it shows up under those filters right away.
  • Cleaner spot descriptions – the automatic descriptions no longer mix in the AI's research notes; you just get the finished, readable text.
✨ Polish
  • Spot rating category labels are now in English.
  • The hourly forecast now explains when no sail size is shown (e.g. add your weight in your profile, or the day is simply too light to plane).

Beta 0.8.5

July 2026

🎽 Sail advisor (your own quiver)
  • The right sail for every hour – the hourly forecast now recommends a sail from your own quiver, based on your weight, the wind and your standard board. Windy can't do this – it doesn't know your board or your sails. Set your weight + a standard board in your profile, and keep your sails in your equipment.
🌡️ Better hourly forecast
  • Each hour now also shows the air temperature and a sky icon (sunny / cloudy / rain) next to wind, direction and gusts.
🏆 Rankings & speed
  • Choose which ranking tables show by default (in the Filter) and load the rest on demand – a lighter, faster start page.
  • Performance & stability improvements under the hood.
🗺️ Spots
  • Spots you create now get an AI description automatically, and you can add a description + country right when creating a spot – so it shows up in the guide immediately.

Beta 0.8.4

July 2026

⭐ Rate the spots
  • Star ratings per spot – rate conditions, infrastructure, ambience, beginner & advanced with a tap (stars only, no text). You see the community average and an overall score.
  • Sort spots by best rating, see the score right in the spot picker, and the overall stars now show on Spot-TV too.
🛡️ Fairer rankings (anti-cheat)
  • Clearly implausible sessions are now kept out of the rankings – impossible speeds/cadence, contradictory data, or tracks that weren't on the water. Honest sessions with noisy GPS stay; if one of yours is excluded, you see exactly why in “View my sessions”.
🆘 Safety check-in (optional)
  • Set a personal safety timer and an emergency e-mail. If you don't check back in (your next upload clears it), your contact is notified. A convenience reminder – not a rescue service.
🗺️ Spots & media
  • Any spot in the world: not just the ones listed – add yours in seconds by typing its name when you log a session (own page, gallery & weather).
  • Create spots in the backoffice (name + coordinates), a webcam preview image that links to the live cam, larger spot photos and WebP uploads.
  • A tidier, collapsible account area (less scrolling, especially on mobile).

Beta 0.8.3

July 2026

⌚ Garmin watch — much easier to get started
  • No more token copying: the watch app now shows a short connect code. Just enter it once on mywatersessions.com (Account → ⌚ Connect watch) to link your sessions to your account. It records from the first minute — nothing gets lost.
  • After every session a confirmation screen shows your upload and links straight to your ranking.
  • New app icon and a clearer recording indicator so you can always tell a session is running.
🗺️ Spot guide — "Who is this spot for?"
  • Spots now carry an orientation card: suitable level (beginner → pro), whether it's a good place to learn, water type (flat / chop / wave), typical wind and things to watch out for (offshore wind, current, rocks…), plus a per-sport matrix. Shown on the spot pages and in the app, with a clear safety disclaimer. It's guidance, not a guarantee.
  • Filter the spot list by "beginner-friendly" and "flat water".
🏆 On the site
  • Highest jump & airtime are now highlighted as ranking categories (wind sports & wakeboard).
  • Time for the distance: the 500 m and nautical-mile rankings now also show the time you needed for that distance – in the tables and directly in the Spot-TV bar.
🆘 Safety check-in (new, optional)
  • Set a personal safety timer and an emergency e-mail in your profile. When you start a session it can arm automatically; if you don't check back in (your next upload disarms it), your contact gets an e-mail. It's a convenience reminder, not a rescue service – no delivery guarantee, and it never replaces a buddy, leash or the coastguard.
📺 Spot-TV & performance
  • Spot-TV now pre-scales to fit a TV (adjustable) and shows the photo gallery in a single tidy row on big screens. The app also got a general speed-up.

Beta 0.8

July 2026

📤 Upload & recording
  • Upload without a Garmin watch: we now accept FIT, GPX and TCX files – from an Apple Watch (e.g. HealthFit), phone GPS apps, Suunto, COROS, or a Garmin Connect / Strava export. Speed, distance, GPS track and your 2 s / 30 s top speeds are computed automatically.
  • New speed disciplines: Best 500 m and Best nautical mile rankings – on the website and on the watch.
  • A short in-app guide shows exactly how to export your session from other apps.
⌚ Garmin watch app
  • Redesigned training screens in the app's look: big current speed, live 2 s and session max.
  • Configurable screens: show/hide pages and switch the big unit (km/h ↔ knots) right from the watch settings.
🗺️ Spots & Spot-TV
  • Spot weather now shows wind, gusts & direction, temperature and sunset.
  • Live webcams embed cleanly (YouTube & co.) and start automatically; plus a spot sponsor banner and a "Sponsored by" credit for local partners.
  • Spot-TV (café / shop screen): record date & board tiles and an auto-refresh safeguard for always-on displays.
🛠️ Backoffice & ads
  • Advertising can be split per sport per spot (e.g. different sponsors for kite vs. windsurf).
  • Rotate gallery photos, a built-in contact / feedback form, and an improved product import that reads more shops' price & image.
🏆 Rankings, filters & personalisation
  • Customisable ranking tables: show/hide and reorder the columns yourself (date, spot, board, sail, weather, trust – and the new fin details). Rank, name and the speed value stay fixed. Save it to your start.
  • Fin details: enter your fin's brand, size and carbon (yes/no) – then filter the rankings by brand, carbon or max size, and show them as columns.
📱 Community & mobile
  • Guest quick-entry: scan the Spot-TV QR code and join today's ranking without an account.
  • Mobile layout optimised: sections stack on phones, no more zoom-in when tapping a field, and ranking tables are shown slimmed (date without the time, km/h only – swipe for more).
  • Spot photo gallery shows up to 6 photos in a tidy grid.
  • Installable web app (PWA), new icons and many small fixes.
